DeFi Dynamics

Current trends indicate that decentralized finance is quickly becoming an important part of the global financial system.
DeFi has the potential to eliminate the need for intermediation and revolutionize the financial sector.

However, in its current state, DeFi is still immature and a developing market. With the growth of this infrastructure, adoption and real-world use cases, DeFi could experience exponential growth.

The main objective of this strategy is to allow investors gaining exposure to a market with great projection but which, in turn, presents highly technical entry barriers.

Decentralized, permissionless and trustless

DeFi aims to be accesible to anyone. Its main goal is to make financial infrastructure available to everyone with a smartphone and an Internet connection.

It does not depend on third parties, contracts or agreements, but on immutable smart contracts. Code is the Law.

No single entity has control over these protocols. DeFi is transparent, has no borders and is not tied to geographic jurisdictions.

Exponential Growth Market

In the future, DeFi could take an important part of the traditional financial sector. It is already a success story in developing countries with low penetration of the banking system.

Selected assets through fundamental analysis

The objective of this strategy is to study the protocols with the best fundamentals and market penetration in order to create a diversified portfolio with long-term exposure to this sector.

Liquid Protocols

It is undeniable that liquidity is an issue. Alphabeth keeps it in mind when constructing its portfolio.

Ethereum Alpha

A solid and evolving technology

Ethereum is the second-largest cryptocurrency by market capitalization after Bitcoin and one of the oldest, dating back to the year 2015. Its developer community is also the largest and most active, consistently enhancing and evolving its technology.

Smart Contracts and DeFi Ecosystem

Ethereum pioneered the implementation of smart contracts through its Ethereum Virtual Machine (EVM), which is used by most protocols. This ecosystem has given rise to the boom in Decentralized Finance (DeFi), enabling a variety of financial applications without intermediaries. The growing adoption of DeFi supports the utility and ongoing demand for Ethereum.

The liquid and scalable blockchain

The Ethereum community remains focused on enhancing the scalability of the network through various technical improvements, such as ETH 2.0. These efforts aim to attract new users and investors by providing solutions that reduce costs and enhance security. Therefore, Ethereum stands out as the blockchain for smart contracts with the highest liquidity.

Cyclical market approach

Our models are based on the economic cycle, similar to Bitcoin. Therefore, we are constantly monitoring market trends, always seeking adaptation to macroeconomic conditions to optimize returns even in adverse scenarios. We have the experience of our of our 5 years track record with the Bitcoin Alpha cell.
